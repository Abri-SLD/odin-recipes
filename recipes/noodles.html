Soy sauce noodles

Simple yet delicious

  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • ½ tablespoon sesame oil
  • ½ tablespoon salt
  • ½ tablespoon black pepper powder
  • 1 package dried noodles
  • 4 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • Half tablespoon sweet soy sauce
  • 1 medium carrot, thinly sliced
  • 3 cayenne peppers
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 ounces dried shrimp (soak it in hot water to soften it)
  • 1 chicken egg
  1. Mix the pepper, dried shrimp and garlics and grind it together until half crushed
  2. Bring a pot of lightly water to boil. Add dried noodles, and cook until al dente, about 5 to 7 minutes. Drain it
  3. Meanwhile, place a saucepan over medium-high heat. Toss cooking oil
  4. Stir in the half crushed garlic, dried shrimp, pepper and chili sauce. Toss carrot and sesame oil
  5. Toss noodles, dark soy sauce and sweet soy sauce. Stir well
  6. Toss chicken egg, add some salt and black pepper powder
  7. Cooks it for another 1 minute
