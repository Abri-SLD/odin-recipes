Curry Fried Rice

    Budget biryani

  • 1 medium carrot, thinly sliced
  • 3 cayenne peppers
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 chicken egg
  • ½ tablespoon sesame oil
  • ½ tablespoon cooking oil
  • 4 tablespoon chili sauce
  • ½ tablespoon salt
  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium heat and cook garlic until fragrant
  2. Stir in carrot and stir-fry until warmed through, about 2 minutes
  3. Add chili sauce and and rice. Stir well. Pour beaten chicken egg on top of the rice
  4. Sprinkle curry, salt and black pepper
