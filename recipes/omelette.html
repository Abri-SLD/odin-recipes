Onion Omelette

The forbidden pizza

  • 2 chicken eggs
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ salt
  • ½ black pepper powder
  • 2 cayenne peppers, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon cooking oil
  1. Whisk ingredients together in a mixing bowl
  2. Heat cooking oil in a skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Pour in the whisked eggs
